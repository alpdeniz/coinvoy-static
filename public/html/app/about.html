Coinvoy is a Bitcoin wallet that is yours and yours only.

Apart from the multisignature technology proven to be the highest security measure in Bitcoin safekeeping, Coinvoy offers 100% independence in Bitcoin ownership. This means that once your wallet is created, Coinvoy Project can never reach your funds nor lose your information to anyone else.

Your addresses and keys are dynamically produced on the fly with the information you provide. Valuable information is instantly destroyed right after your transaction is built and signed.

Coinvoy users can store, spend, collect or distribute their Bitcoin funds with a piece of mind, knowing that even the collapse of all Coinvoy Project will NOT effect their Bitcoin funds
