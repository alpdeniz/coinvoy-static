What is Bitcoin?
Bitcoin is a decentralized online currency, in which no regulative institution exists. Bitcoins virtually exist in a public ledger system, called the blockchain. You can receive, store and send funds to anywhere in the world. They can be utilized by shopping in online merchants, or by exchanging them to local currencies through Bitcoin ATMs or exchange platforms in the country of your bank account.
What is the catch here?

How secure is this wallet?
We do not have any word on your wallet. Even if our servers are comprimised you have no risk of losing your coins. If servers are gone away, you can still reach your Bitcoins by using a simple offline script we provide. An unusually strong encryption is employed on your browser which generates your most secret keys on-the-fly when they are needed. And all of it luckily happens very very fast, thanks to AsmCryptoJS library.
Can I exchange my Bitcoins here?
No. However, you can always transfer desired amount of funds to any online exhange platform of your preference and exchange them there.
